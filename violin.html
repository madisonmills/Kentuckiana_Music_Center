Violin!

The Violin, or more commonly known in Bluegrass Music, the Fiddle, is a four stringed instrument. The standard tuning of a violin from top to bottom is G, D, A, E. The violin is often an easily identifiable instrument in music.


There is a prominent fiddle player from Henryville, Indiana named Michael Cleveland. Cleveland is blind but has excelled at the fiddle and won his first grammy in 2018.
