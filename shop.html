Shop

The best way to browse our shop would be to come in the store and check out our options! Getting to pick up the instruments for yourself and play them before you buy and ask any questions is highly recommended.

While you are here, we can also service your instrument(s). This includes sizing, cleaning, restringing, and tuning. In addition, our team can also repair new or old instruments.
