Quality Assurance

We work very closely with Amati's Fine Instruments out of Cincinnati, Howard Core, and with Lane & Edwards Violins out of Louisville, KY. All instruments are expertly adjusted. All of our rental instruments include the following:

  • Adjustment of bridge, sound post, pegs and bow.
  • Bow with real horse hair.
  • High quality case with attach nylon cover, or padded nylon bag for cello/bass.
  • Perlon type strings.
  • Tailpiece with 4 fine tuners.
  • Appropriate rosin.
